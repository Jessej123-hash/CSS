Nelson Mandela

Early life

Nelson Mandela was born in Mvezo in South Africa to Gadla Mphakanyishwa and Noqaphi Nosekeni.He left his village and went to study at Fort Hare university which influenced his desire to fight for the African people

Achievements

  • Joined the Africa National Congress and formed the military wing"umkhonto we sizwe
  • Won the Nobel Peace Prize in 1993 along with Frederick de Klerk
  • Fought for the abolishment of the apartheid system
